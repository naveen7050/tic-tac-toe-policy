Tic Tac Toe is a free, ad-supported mobile game developed and published by Red-F Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected and used when you use the app.

Information Collection

The app does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the app uses third-party advertising services, specifically Google AdMob, to display advertisements. These services automatically collect certain information to serve ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.

  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Device identifiers such as Android Advertising ID
  • Ad interaction data (views and clicks)
  • Device and performance diagnostics

Red-F Studios does not directly access, store, or control this data. All such information is processed according to Google’s privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

This app integrates Google Play Services and Google AdMob. We encourage users to review Google’s privacy policy for detailed information about how data is handled.

Log Data

In the event of a crash or technical error, diagnostic information may be automatically collected by third-party services. This may include IP address, device model, operating system version, app configuration, and timestamps. We do not personally collect or store this information.

Data Retention

The app does not use user accounts or a proprietary backend system. Therefore, we do not have the technical ability to identify individual users or delete specific user data. Any data collected through third-party advertising services is managed according to their own data retention policies.

Age and Audience

This app has been rated as suitable for general audiences based on the Google Play content rating questionnaire. However, in Google Play’s target audience settings, the app is designated for users aged 18 and older.

The app does not contain adult content and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and become effective immediately upon posting.

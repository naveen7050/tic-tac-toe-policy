Payment and Transaction Data

When you purchase virtual items, coin packages, or premium upgrades within Tic Tac Toe, all financial transactions are managed and executed entirely by Google Play Billing.

  • What We Do Not See: Red-F Studios does not directly access, see, process, or store your credit card numbers, bank account numbers, or primary billing credentials.

Red-F Studios is not responsible for coin balance loss resulting from device changes, app uninstallation, or data clearing.

The app displays advertisements using Google AdMob. These services may automatically collect certain parameters to show relevant ads and measure performance:

  • Red-F Studios does not directly manage or store this data. Information gathered by advertising providers is handled in accordance with their own privacy policies.

This app integrates the following third-party services, each of which maintains independent privacy documentation:
